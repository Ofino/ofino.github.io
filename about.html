Ofino is a small, focused analytics consultancy that helps teams make sense of their data — building pipelines, dashboards, and reporting infrastructure that turns raw information into clear, actionable decisions.


Our Mission

Our mission is simple: help teams understand what's happening, why it matters, and what to do next. We focus on building clean, reliable data infrastructure so decisions are grounded in insight and execution doesn't stall.


What We Do

We provide analytics and data consulting across three areas:

  • Dashboard Design & Development – Building views that surface the right information for the right audience, structured around the decisions teams actually need to make
  • Data Pipeline & Reporting Infrastructure – Connecting sources, cleaning data, and building the workflows that keep reporting accurate and repeatable
  • Analytics & Performance Measurement – Helping teams define what to measure, how to capture it, and how to make it visible across stakeholders

Why Ofino?

  • Decision-First Approach – We start with the question, not the data. Every dashboard is built around what someone needs to do differently based on what they see
  • Clean, Usable Outputs – No unnecessary complexity. One source of truth, structured for the people acting on it
  • AI-Assisted Workflows – We incorporate automation and AI tooling to handle the mechanical parts faster, so more time goes toward analysis and strategy
  • Media & Agency Experience – We understand how data works in campaign-driven environments where accuracy, speed, and stakeholder trust all matter
